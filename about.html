About Me

I am a computer scientist, mathematician, and tutor with 7 years of experience. I live in Portland, Oregon and attend Portland State University. I have worked as a private tutor and small group class instructor, teaching high school and college level mathematics and computer science. I love teaching, and my teaching is inspired and motivated by my love of programming, CS theory, and mathematical methods and theories. I hope that, should you choose to reach out to me, we can foster a similar passion in whatever subject(s) you need or want help with!
